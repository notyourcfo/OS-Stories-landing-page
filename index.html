Open Source Stories

An open-source project to capture the stories of exceptional builders - as short, watchable reels.

We make short-form videos that feel human:
the late-night bug fixes, the awkward first pitches, the tiny wins that mean everything.
The goal is simple -- surface honest, cinematic slices of people actually building stuff so others can see how messy the stuff could be.

How it works

This is open for anyone doing interesting work. There are two ways to get featured:

  • Make it yourself: we share the format and tips so you can create a reel in our style.
  • We make it for you: we handle scripting, edits, and post it with you as a collaborator.

To set the tone we're publishing a few early stories produced by our team - honest, rough, and real. After that, the project is open: if your story fits the vibe, we'll work with you.

Who we're looking for

Builders, makers, founders, creators, ppl trying something weird and unusual.
A quick example: “I built a AI powered toy which works without internet connection”
That's the kind of human story we love to hear.

Submit your story →

We read every submission. Short, specific answers that include one true, weird moment increase your chance of getting featured.

FAQ (quick)

Do I need video experience? No. If you choose "we make it for you" we'll guide the shoot and do the edit.

Will I be credited? Always - you'll be tagged as a collaborator on the post.

Is there a fee? Not initially - we're building out the archive and experimenting with formats. If anything changes we'll be transparent up front.
